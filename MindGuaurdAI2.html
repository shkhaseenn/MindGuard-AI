Real Help is Always Available

Emergency Care

If you are experiencing immediate thoughts of suicide or self-harm, please reach out to a professional human crisis worker immediately. You do not have to carry this alone.
United States

Call or text 988 to reach the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline, available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Services are free and confidential.
International Support

Please contact your national emergency services or visit Befrienders Worldwide to find immediate support near you.
